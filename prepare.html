THE PROJECT

Data+AI is a free tutorial website aimed at teaching the basics of data analysis while utilising simple AI to optimise your workflow. It is aimed at beginners and will break down the entire process into 6 stages:

  1. Plan
  2. Prepare
  3. Process
  4. Analyse
  5. Share
  6. Act

An example project will be used to demonstrate all the necessary skills. This will be a musical trend analysis using Kanye West lyrics and sales figures. By the end of the project, you will be able to predict the success of an album by determining the most frequently used words in each track. You will learn to create word clouds like this one on the right (in the shape of the Gradutation Bear). This website is a jumping off point to learn these skills and then take them further on your own projects. It is a resource you can return to in any order to refresh your memory.

A Data Analyst is a rewarding and sought-after career and the more prior education you have will allow you to build an enticing portfolio that demonstrates your personal skills to prospective employers. Even if you are not seeking a career in this field directly, many jobs require conducting rudimentary data analysis to make sound business decisions.

It is assumed you are familiar with the concepts of data analysis and artificial intelligence, along with essential computer skills such as downloading programs and using chat bots.

TECHNOLOGY

Many of you may be familiar with data analysis through the use of spreadsheet programs such as Microsoft Excel. However, in the current information age, the amount of data created and consumed is increasing exponentially. Many companies now utilise big data in their analysis. In order to process large amount of information more efficiently, coding is used to simplify and automate the process. AI is used to streamline this process even further. For these reasons, this website will focus on the following software:

  • Python
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • Visual Studio Code
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
  • ChatGPT
